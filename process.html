Maximizing Productivity, Uptime, and Operational Efficiency

In asset-intensive industries, where equipment, facilities, and structures form the core of your operations, a robust and multi-layered Process Integrity Management system is essential. At GIKO ENGINEERING LIMITED, we prioritize a modern, integrated approach to safeguard your operational assets, ensuring ongoing reliability and cost-efficiency.

Our Process Integrity Management system emphasizes enhancing operational performance, safety, and asset longevity through detailed risk assessments, root cause investigations, and comprehensive evaluations of your process plant equipment. We help you maintain your assets in optimal, fit-for-service condition, minimizing downtime and operational risks.

Our Process Integrity Management Services

  • Integrity & Maintenance Evaluation: Assessing work processes for static pressurised equipment and marine structures to ensure integrity and optimal maintenance management.
  • Procedural & Management Documentation: Delivering comprehensive integrity management plans and procedures for efficient operations.
  • Organizational Assessments: Evaluating your company's structure and processes to sustain integrity and maintenance excellence.
  • Risk Analysis & Root Cause Investigation: Providing detailed reports based on thorough understanding of process equipment, corrosion mechanisms, and material considerations.
  • Long-term Integrity Program Development: Crafting bespoke, strategic programs aimed at extending equipment lifespan and maintaining safety standards.
  • Degradation & Service Life Calculations: Analyzing equipment wear and tear to optimize lifecycle management.
  • Corrosion Engineering & Root Cause Analysis: Identifying corrosion issues at their source and implementing targeted mitigation strategies.
  • CUI (Corrosion Under Insulation) Management: Developing specialized programmes to prevent corrosion caused by external moisture, a persistent challenge in the industry.
  • Barrier Management & Accident Prevention: Establishing effective barrier systems to mitigate major safety risks and protect critical assets.

Focused CUI Management Strategy

Despite ongoing advances in Asset Integrity Management, CUI remains a major challenge, costing companies millions annually. Our risk-based inspection and mitigation strategies, integrated into our comprehensive evaluation and condition assessment programmes, enable you to target your efforts precisely—so you can keep your core operations running smoothly and safely.
