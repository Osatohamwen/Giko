Optimizing the Integrity of Your Structures

Structural Integrity Management (SIM) is vital for ensuring the safety of personnel and assets on offshore units, which are frequently subjected to harsh conditions like massive waves, extreme cold, or storms.

At GIKO ENGINEERING LIMITED, we recognize the complexities involved in monitoring and maintaining the integrity of your offshore installations. Our SIM process involves a comprehensive assessment of structural design, fabrication quality, technical documentation, and inspection procedures. This thorough evaluation helps identify potential weak points and develop effective mitigation measures.

Our aim is to achieve optimal results by harmonizing practical field efforts with detailed integrity assessments conducted in the office. Combining solid theoretical expertise and extensive hands-on experience, we utilize collected data to accurately evaluate the current condition of your structures.

Our Full Range of Structural Integrity Services Includes:

  • Defining performance standards and developing strategic inspection protocols
  • Designing and modifying long-term inspection programs
  • Analyzing inspection findings, conducting root cause investigations, and recommending repairs or modifications
  • Reviewing inspection, repair, and modification history to assess current structural integrity
  • Quantifying uncertainties in structural analysis, inspection quality, and detailed design processes
  • Conducting structural reliability assessments, fatigue analysis, and structural monitoring
  • Managing structural certification processes and ensuring compliance with regulatory standards
  • Performing lifetime extension studies for aging assets

Supporting Services

Our structural integrity engineers are complemented by specialists providing additional related services such as:

  • Hull and structural inspections
  • Conventional and advanced Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) methods
  • Quality assurance and quality control (QA/QC) during fabrication processes
