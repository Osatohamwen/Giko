Your Partner in Ensuring Regulatory Compliance, Safe Lifting Operations, and Asset Integrity

Crane and Lifting Integrity refers to the comprehensive assurance that all crane systems and lifting equipment are operating safely, structurally reliable, well-maintained, and fully compliant with applicable industry regulations. It encompasses regular inspections, testing, maintenance, and engineering assessments to confirm that the equipment performs as intended without posing risks to personnel, assets, or operations. This integrity-focused approach is essential for ensuring safe lifting practices and preventing failures in demanding industrial environments.

At GIKO ENGINEERING LTD, we understand that the safety and reliability of cranes and lifting appliances are vital for protecting personnel and optimizing asset performance. As a trusted independent specialist in the field of cranes and lifting appliances, we deliver comprehensive solutions for safe and compliant lifting operations.

Our multidisciplinary engineering team offers expert support across all aspects of crane and lifting appliance integrity�including strength calculations, control systems, electrical and hydraulic systems, compliance assurance, maintenance, and more. We are dedicated to providing tailored guidance, robust assessments, and strategic advice to ensure operational excellence.

Our Core Services Include:

  • Crane and Lifting Equipment Integrity Management
  • Design Verification and Strength Calculations
  • Regulatory Compliance & GAP Analysis
  • Risk Assessments & Feasibility Studies
  • Incident Investigations & Root Cause Analysis
  • Third-party and Client-focused Compliance Audits
  • Lifetime & Condition Assessments
  • Engineering Support & Advisory Services
  • Development of Crane Specifications
  • Safe Lifting Operations Support & Planning
