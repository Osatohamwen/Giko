About Us

Giko Engineering is a well-established, wholly Nigerian-owned company with a strategic focus on the oil & gas, petrochemical, and energy sectors. We specialize in providing high-quality engineering solutions and are a trusted supplier of specialized industrial products for maintenance and technical services across West Africa and beyond.

Our reputation is built on consistent delivery, industry expertise, and a commitment to excellence. From critical infrastructure support to routine industrial maintenance, Giko Engineering partners with clients to ensure seamless operations and optimized performance.
