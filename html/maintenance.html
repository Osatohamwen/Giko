Expert Maintenance Management Services

Maintenance Management is the systematic process of planning, coordinating, and executing maintenance activities to ensure assets, equipment, and infrastructure operate reliably and efficiently. At GIKO ENGINEERING LIMITED, we offer end-to-end maintenance management solutions tailored to diverse operational environments and client needs.

With deep industry expertise and a proven track record, our team develops data-driven maintenance strategies that enhance asset performance, ensure regulatory compliance, and give organizations full visibility and control over their critical assets.

Integrated Engineering Framework

Our skilled maintenance engineers have developed a robust and scalable maintenance engineering framework applicable across sectors including energy, maritime, and industrial operations. Our solutions aim to maximize reliability, optimize costs, and prolong asset lifecycle through:

  • Proactive maintenance planning
  • Predictive diagnostics
  • Real-time performance monitoring

Bridge the Maintenance Gap — Achieve Peak Performance

By adopting a proactive, analytical approach, we help clients meet international standards and maintain optimal asset performance throughout their lifecycle. Our methodologies include:

  • Reliability Centered Maintenance (RCM)
  • Failure Mode, Effects, and Criticality Analysis (FMECA)
  • Root Cause Analysis (RCA)

These techniques allow us to:

  • Prioritize maintenance based on equipment criticality
  • Define long-term maintenance strategies
  • Support condition-based maintenance (CBM)
  • Recommend optimized maintenance routines
  • Identify design improvements to reduce maintenance needs
  • Create a solid spare parts management strategy
  • Ensure compliance with industry regulations and standards

Risk Management and Hazard Analysis

Our multidisciplinary team conducts thorough risk assessments to systematically identify hazards, operational risks, and failure causes. We implement effective risk mitigation strategies using globally recognized methodologies, such as:

  • Hazard Identification (HAZID)
  • Hazard and Operability Studies (HAZOP)
  • Fault Tree Analysis (FTA)

Through this comprehensive and intelligent approach, GIKO ENGINEERING LIMITED empowers your organization to achieve greater safety, efficiency, and reliability across all maintenance activities.
