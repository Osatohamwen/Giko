Maximizing Productivity, Uptime, and Operational Efficiency

Total Rig Integrity Management involves a thorough and structured strategy aimed at maintaining the safety, dependability, and efficiency of every aspect and operation of a drilling rig, whether it is located offshore or onshore.

GIKO ENGINEERING LIMITED's Total Rig Integrity Management (TRIM) approach consolidates key scopes into a cohesive, cost-effective package designed to optimize inspection, certification, and verification processes.

Our systematic evaluation of assets, continuous performance monitoring, and strict adherence to regulatory standards enable us to help you achieve your operational and business objectives efficiently.

We prioritize streamlining maintenance workflows to minimize mobilizations, enhance rig performance, and reduce unnecessary costs and downtime.

Our TRIM program is specifically tailored for the offshore industry, with core objectives including:

  • Minimizing risk and preventing asset failures
  • Ensuring full compliance with health, safety, and environmental (HSE) standards
  • Maintaining the integrity of safety barrier functions
  • Implementing fleet-wide, digital, and standardized solutions
  • Meeting current and future statutory and corporate requirements
  • Enhancing financial outcomes by optimizing operations within a fixed, predictable budget
