Lifting Appliances

We inspect cranes, hoists, and other lifting gear to ensure operational safety and compliance.

Our detailed inspections include load testing and certification aligned with international standards like LOLER and API.

Drilling Equipment

Comprehensive inspections for top drives, rotary tables, BOPs, and handling tools.

We evaluate the mechanical integrity, wear, and performance to ensure safe drilling operations in high-risk environments.

Static Pressurized Equipment

Inspection and assessment of vessels, pipelines, and heat exchangers.

We use ultrasonic, radiographic, and visual methods to detect corrosion, cracking, and material loss under pressure conditions.

Hull and Structures

Marine and offshore structure inspections for fatigue, corrosion, and integrity.

Our certified inspectors carry out thickness measurements, structural checks, and damage assessments using class-approved techniques.

Storage Tanks

Inspection of fixed and floating roof tanks for product storage and environmental compliance.

We carry out MFL, vacuum box, and API 653 visual inspections to assess floor corrosion, shell deformation, and seal condition.

Pressure Safety Valves

Inspection, calibration, and certification of safety relief valves.

We ensure proper set pressure, leakage integrity, and compliance to ASME/API standards through on-site or workshop calibration.

Instrument Calibration

Calibration and verification of instruments for flow, pressure, and temperature.

We use certified master instruments to adjust and verify sensors and transmitters, ensuring process accuracy and compliance.

Jacking System

Calibration and verification of instruments for flow, pressure, and temperature.

We use certified master instruments to adjust and verify sensors and transmitters, ensuring process accuracy and compliance.

Dropped Object Management

Identification and mitigation of potential dropped object hazards.

We carry out surveys, securement assessments, and implement tagging systems to minimize risk to personnel and equipment.

Non-Destructive Testing (NDT)

Advanced NDT techniques to assess material and weld integrity without damage.

We provide UT, MT, PT, RT, and phased array testing to detect flaws, cracks, and corrosion in critical assets.
